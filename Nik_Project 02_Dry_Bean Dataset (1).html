Handling Imbalanced Data¶

Here 2 fundamental ways of handling imbalance data:

                                          A)Undersampling

                                          B)Oversampling

*Imbalanced data always handeled in the target column in a dataset. "IF A dataset is in ratio of 60:40 then it is considered as imbalanced"

UNDERSAMPLING¶

"In this technique we drop the records of majority class randomly to match (to bring it down) to the ratio of 60:40 between the 2 output classes"

Ex. A dataset having 900 yes's and 100 no's.

So, here we randomly drop 800 records of yes class to bring it in ratio of 60:40.
